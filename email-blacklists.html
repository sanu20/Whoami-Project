How to obtain the email headers?

This is different for each email provider. For example, in gmail, you click on the 'More' button on the top right corner of your email and choose the 'Show original' option to view the email source including its headers. See https://mxtoolbox.com/public/content/emailheaders/ for detailed steps to obtain email headers for different email providers.
